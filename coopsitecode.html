About The Work

With this contract, I was given the reigns to lead in design implementation and to help collaborate on the redesign and re-branding. Working with the team at BlueSpruce, we completely re-branded Innovation Pet into Coops & Feathers. Their e-commerce site needed to reflect these major changes and reinvent their brand identity to pivot from a wide variety of products to a more refined, core foundation of products to represent the Coops & Feathers brand.
