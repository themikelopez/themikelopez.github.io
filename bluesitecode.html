About The Work

With is project, I assisted in logo development and brand redesign. Working alongside their lead developer and designer, I was able to see the process of a website overhaul from the ground up. We got direction from the client for the logo and settled upon the sprig of the blue spruce tree.

The next step we took was to develop a color palate and design style we wanted to implement. Considering UX in our decision, we opted for a nature/forest theme and tones that reflected our welcoming, yet professional goal for the site. Searching through the Adobe Stock image database and Unsplash, we were able to get some ideas for imagery we wanted to populate the site with. Creating assets that reflected the marketing goals of BlueSpruce, we started to really see the skeleton of the new site come together.

Working with the founder of BlueSpruce, we constructed copy to help tell their story and convey their services to gain new clients, all while showcasing past work they had in their portfolio.

Towards the end of this project, my workload while operating a small business (Tree Line Candle Co.) increased dramatically and I had to step back and focus on production to meet holiday demand. While I was away, I stayed in contact with the team and followed the wrapping up of the site. More than satisfied with the end product, the founder of BlueSpruce gave the green light on launching the site. This is currently a live website that you can visit and explore.
